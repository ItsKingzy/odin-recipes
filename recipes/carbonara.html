Carbonara

Description

Carbonara is an easy to make dish crafted with a few simple ingredients that you might already have in your fridge or pantry! It's the perfect meal for a date night, a high-protein meal after the gym, or a lunch for the office.

Ingredients

  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 1 cup of 1% milk
  • 1/3 cup grated pecorino romano/li>
  • 1/3 cup grated parmesan
  • 1/3 cup bacon bits
  • 2 tsp ground pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 350g of spaghetti
