Carbonara

Description

Carbonara is an easy to make dish crafted with a few simple ingredients that you might already have in your fridge or pantry! It's the perfect meal for a date night, a high-protein meal after the gym, or a lunch for the office.

Ingredients

  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 1 cup of 1% milk
  • 1/3 cup grated pecorino romano/li>
  • 1/3 cup grated parmesan
  • 1/3 cup bacon bits
  • 2 tsp ground pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 350g of spaghetti

Steps

  1. Prepare a large pot with around 4-6 cups of water inside. Place your 350g of spaghetti in the pot and heat on high. Let boil while you prepare other food.
  2. In the mean time, start grating your cheeses. Grate 1/3 cup of pecorino romano and set aside, do the same for 1/3 cup parmesan.
  3. In a small mixing bowl, add in your 2 egg yolks and whisk until you achieve a consistent yellow colour.
  4. Then, add in 1 cup of 1% milk and mix well with a fork or whisk.
  5. Then, add in your grated cheeses (1/3 cup grated pecorino romano and 1/3 cup grated parmesan) from the earlier steps. Mix well with a fork or whisk.
  6. Finally, in the same bowl, add in a bit of ground black pepper (to your liking) to add some extra flavour. Set aside for now.
  7. Check on your spaghetti, by now the spaghetti should almost be prepared to used. So, drain most of your spaghetti water, but set aside a small portion (about a cup).
  8. In the time being, heat a pan on medium heat and add in your 1/3 cup bacon bits and begin cooking them.
  9. Once you have obtained a desired crispiness to the bacon, add your spaghetti into the pan alongside the small portion of spaghetti water you saved earlier. Mix well.
  10. Subsequently, adjust the heat setting for the pan to low add gradually add in your cheese sauce you made ealier to the pan and mix constantly.
  11. NOTE:you don't want to make a clumpy cheese sauce in the pan, so once you see parts of the sauce start to clump up, immediately take the pan off from the heat and place carbonara into a seperate serving dish.

And just like that! You have successfully made a super delicious dish, perfect for any occassion. Enjoy!
