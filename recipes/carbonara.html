Carbonara

Description

Carbonara is an easy to make dish crafted with a few simple ingredients that you might already have in your fridge or pantry! It's the perfect meal for a date night, a high-protein meal after the gym, or a lunch for the office.

Ingredients

  • 6 Yukon Gold/Golden potatoes
  • 1/4 cup 1% milk
  • 1/4 cup half/half cream
  • 1 cup salted chicken broth
  • 1 cup sharp cheedar cheese
  • 1 cup gruyere cheese
  • 2-3 tbsp flour
  • 4 tbsp salted butter
  • Optionally 1 white onion
  • Seasonings (to your disression):
    • Garlic powder
    • Onion powder
    • Paparika
    • Salt
    • Pepper
