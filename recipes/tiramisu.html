Tiramisu

Description

This delectable dessert is both delicious and easy to make! It's perfect for a fancy dinner at home or a way to quickly satisfy your sweetness cravings from home. Let's get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 1 package JELL-O Vanilla Pudding mix
  • 250g low-fat marscarpone
  • 1/4 cup coffee liqueur
  • 1 tbsp Maxwell House Instant Coffee granules
  • 1/2 cup hot water
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 package ladyfingers (~200g)
  • 1/2 - 1 cup Cool Whip whipped topping
  • 4 squares Bakers Semi-Sweet Chocolate, coarsely grated

Steps

  1. Prepare the Vanilla JELL-O according to its package's directions either by microwave or by stovetop. Then whisk marscarpone cheese and coffee liqueur into the hot JELL-O mixture.
  2. Cover the JELL-O mixture with plastic wrap and let cool for an hour.
  3. Next, combine coffee granules, hot water, and sugar in a mixing bowl. Brush over the ladyfingers.
  4. After preparing the ladyfingers, fold the whipped topping into the pudding mixture.
  5. Line the bottom of an 8 cup trifle bowl with half of the ladyfingers you prepared earlier.
  6. Then, evenly spread half of the pudding mixture over the ladyfingers.
  7. Once you're satisfied with the spread of the pudding mixture, sprinkle half of the grated chocolate evenly over the pudding spread.
  8. Repeat the last 4 steps for the second half of the ingredients.
  9. Cover the bowl tightly with plastic wrap and chill the dessert for 4 hours (or overnight) to blend in the flavours.
