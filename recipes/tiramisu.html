Tiramisu

Description

This delectable dessert is both delicious and easy to make! It's perfect for a fancy dinner at home or a way to quickly satisfy your sweetness cravings from home. Let's get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 1 package JELL-O Vanilla Pudding mix
  • 250g low-fat marscarpone
  • 1/4 cup coffee liqueur
  • 1 tbsp Maxwell House Instant Coffee granules
  • 1/2 cup hot water
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 package ladyfingers (~200g)
  • 1/2 - 1 cup Cool Whip whipped topping
  • 4 squares Bakers Semi-Sweet Chocolate, coarsely grated
