Potatoes au Gratin

Description

This delicious dish is the perfect dish for any pasta lover that loves a fast, low-effort meal packed with a rich flavour of mixed cheeses.

This delicious dish is the perfect dish for any potato enjoyer. It makes a perfect dish for an eventful night with family and friends.

Packing a load of carbs, a variety of cheeses, and several spices, your cravings will surely be met!

Ingredients

  • 6 Yukon Gold/Golden potatoes
  • 1/4 cup 1% milk
  • 1/4 cup half/half cream
  • 1 cup salted chicken broth
  • 1 cup sharp cheedar cheese
  • 1 cup gruyere cheese
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 4 tbsp salted butter
  • 1 white onion
  • Seasonings (to your disression):
    • Garlic powder
    • Onion powder
    • Paparika
    • Salt
    • Pepper
